Key Features

SunSync combines IoT technology with thoughtful design to create a natural light management system that works for you.

Real-time Weather Tracking

Access live weather data including accurate sunrise and sunset times to plan your day around natural light patterns.

IoT Light Monitoring

Connect to Arduino MKR1010 light sensors to monitor and track real-time indoor light levels throughout your day.

Smart Activity Reminders

Set activity reminders synced with sunrise, sunset, or optimal light conditions to maintain a healthy, nature-aligned routine.

Light History Visualization

View beautiful charts showing your light exposure throughout the day, helping you identify patterns and optimize your environment.

Smart Recommendations

Receive personalized activity and environment suggestions based on current light levels and weather conditions.

Cloud Synchronization

All your data is safely stored in the cloud, allowing you to access your light history and reminders across multiple devices.

Designed for Natural Living

Modern lifestyles often disconnect us from natural light cycles, leading to disrupted sleep patterns, reduced productivity, and mood disorders. SunSync helps you reconnect with nature's rhythm.

By tracking both outdoor and indoor light conditions, SunSync creates a personalized schedule that helps you optimize your activities based on natural light availability — improving focus, energy levels, and overall wellbeing.

How SunSync Works

From setup to daily use, SunSync integrates seamlessly into your routine.

1

Connect IoT Sensor

Set up your Arduino MKR1010 with the light sensor in your home or workspace.

2

Track Light Patterns

The app automatically collects and analyzes both outdoor and indoor light data.

3

Set Smart Reminders

Create activity reminders based on sunrise, sunset, or optimal light conditions.

4

Optimize Your Day

Follow personalized recommendations to improve your well-being through natural light.
